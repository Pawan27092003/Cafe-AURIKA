Our Story

A Garden Retreat in the City

Café Aurika is Gurugram's beloved garden café — where the ambience calms you the moment you step in, freshly brewed coffee greets you warmly, and every dish feels like a story.

Nestled in Sushant Lok Phase I, our outdoor seating transports you miles away from the city's hustle. We serve an eclectic mix of Indian and Italian cuisine — from soul-warming pastas to hand-tossed pizzas and specialty coffees that make mornings worth it.
